Game Rules

(Truth or Dare) or Shot

  • Every person will receive a playing card when they get their first drink.
  • You should not get more than one.
  • On each card will be either a question or a task (cough truth or dare cough).
  • When you meet someone (new or old), you can ask them to answer or do what is written on your card. They should do the same to you.
  • Failure to do so results in a penalty shot.
  • Upon completion, exchange playing cards.
  • Tom do we win anything? Yes, friendship

  • Every half hour, Tom will draw a card. Everyone who has that suit or rank drinks with Tom. If he draws a Joker, everyone drinks
